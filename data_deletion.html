User Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data from the FitLife App, please follow the instructions below:

Option 1: In-App Request

If available, go to the app settings and select "Delete My Account". This will remove your profile and associated data.

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to support@fitlifeapp.dev with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include your registered email address and any relevant details to help us identify your account.

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm once your data has been deleted.
